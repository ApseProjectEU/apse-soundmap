🎧 APSE Sound Map

How to add sounds:

  1. Click anywhere on the map
  2. Fill in the sound details
  3. Paste your Archive.org audio link (any format works)
  4. Select sound category
  5. Click Save — your marker appears!

Archive.org links:

  • https://archive.org/details/collection/file.mp3
  • https://archive.org/download/collection/file.mp3
Both formats work automatically! The map converts any Archive.org link into a working embedded player.

Project markers:

  • Locked markers (🔒) are official APSE project recordings and cannot be modified.
  • Your personal markers can be edited or deleted anytime.
